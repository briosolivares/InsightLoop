# Rough Idea: Insight Loop

A local AI voice agent that helps founders or entrepreneurs conduct primary market research interviews and convert conversations into structured insights.

## Core Concept

The goal is to assist with the end-to-end primary market research process:

1. **ICP Definition** — The founder creates an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) describing who their target customer is.
2. **Customer Qualification** — Identify and qualify potential customers who fit the ICP.
3. **Outreach** — Reach out to qualified prospects to set up primary market research interviews.
4. **Interview / Transcription** — Conduct conversations and get them transcribed.
5. **Insight Extraction** — Extract key insights from transcriptions and generate a structured summary report.
6. **Decision Support** — Use the report to inform strategic decisions about where to take the company.

## Key Qualities

- Local AI (runs on the user's machine)
- Voice-enabled agent (can conduct or assist with interviews)
- Structured output (insights and reports, not raw transcripts)
- Founder/entrepreneur focused (non-technical user persona)
